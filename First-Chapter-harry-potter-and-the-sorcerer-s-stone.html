📚 Help & Guides

📖 Three Learning Modes

  • Flashcard: Flip cards to reveal definitions. Click tabs to see both sentences.
  • Quiz (Group): Two-part test: choose the word, then spell it. Auto-stars words you miss.
  • Mastered Words: Quiz only your starred words (after ⭐ 5+ words).

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ←/→ Previous/Next word
  • ↑/↓ Previous/Next group
  • S Toggle star
  • P Pronounce word
  • 1/2 Switch sentence tab

💡 Study Tips

  • Use Flashcard mode to get familiar with words
  • Star words you find tricky for targeted review
  • Read both sentences to see the word in different contexts
  • Use Pronunciation to practice speaking
  • Take the Group Quiz to test your knowledge
